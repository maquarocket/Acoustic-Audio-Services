About Me

About Me

Get to know about me and what I do

My name is Jeremy Lau. I am a student audio engineer based in Melbourne, Australia. I have an interest and background in both arts and sciences. I am mainly a cover artist and have played in multiple cover bands for 4 years. At the same time, my education background primarily lies in science and engineering, having previously studies software engineering.

My business is all about providing quality acoustic services that are affordable, while engaging with my customers. I long to spread the importance of good acoustics in the music industry to younger or less experienced practitioners. However, I also desire to improve the quality of life for the greater public by applying my acoustical knowledge in the service of other people.
